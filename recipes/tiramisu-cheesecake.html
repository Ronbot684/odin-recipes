No-Bake Tiramisu Cheesecake

Description

A wonderfully delicious recipe making a tiramisu cheesecake without the use of any baking. Very coffee flavored, so if that's your thing, this is for you. Takes about 7 hours to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons of instant espresso coffee powder, divided
  • 6 tablespoons of coffee liqueur, divided
  • 2 (8-ounce) packages of mascarpone cheese, softened
  • 2 (8-ounce) packages of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 (7-ounce) package of crisp Italian ladyfingers (savoiardi), divided
  • 1 cup of sliced almonds, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • ⅓ cup of butter, melted
  • 2 cups of whipping cream
  • unsweetened cocoa powder for dusting, as needed
  • (optional) 12 chocolate covered espresso beans
